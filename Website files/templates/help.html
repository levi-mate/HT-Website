Advance booking

You can save money by booking your tickets in advance.


The following rates apply:

Between 80 and 90 days in advance: 20% discount

Between 60 and 79 days in advance: 10% discount

Between 45 and 59 days in advance: 5% discount


The discount applies to the total booking price.

Managing your booking

You can manage your bookings in two ways:

1, by going into the menu on the left hand side of the top of the page, then selecting the "My Trips" option.

2, by going into the menu on the left hand side of the top of the page, then selecting the "Account" option, then selecting the "My Trips" option.

Cancellations

Booking cancellation before 60 days of booking date does not incur cancellation charges.

Booking cancellation between 30 and 60 days of booking date will incur charges up to 50% of booking price.

Within 30 days of booking date 100% of booking price will be charged.

Managing your account

You can manage your account by going into the menu on the left hand side of the top of the page, then selecting the "Account" option.

There you can access any options associated with your account.
