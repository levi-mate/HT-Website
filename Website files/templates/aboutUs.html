Horizon Travels

The company was founded in 1907 by the eccentric visionary, Ravels T. Horizon.

He was trying to plan a journey to the north, and he quickly realised the limitations of his time.

No matter where and how hard he looked, he just could not find a reputable company that could help arrange his upcoming trip!

So he did what every billionaire seems to do when they are slightly inconvenienced and decided to throw money at the problem.

Thus, his new company, Horizon Travels was created.


Through the decades since then, we have strived to be the best travel booking company in all the UK.

Slowly but surely, we have expanded across the entire country, and nowadays we offer a variety of journey options for those with adventure in their heart.


Our continued commitment to excellent customer service and great reliability has won us a number of awards over the years.

Recently we have been named the UK's leading travel booking company based on customer satisfaction.

When you travel with us, you can rest easy knowing that you are in good hands!
